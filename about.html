CognEye

Upholding the ability to percieve!

CognEye is a non-profit AI platform, developed by students of VJTI Mumbai, India to help the visually impaired community to get a more better and clear understanding of their surroundings and conceive the world a better to live in.

CognEye platform will help detect a person's surrounding through its AI features like Object Detection, Optical Character Recognition, Location Detection, facial Recognition, etc.

Our team is currently working on adding more features in the application and making it more efficient.

Our Objective:

In India, its estimated that, 62 million people suffer from visual ability, out of which 54 millions have limited vision problems. CognEye aims to assist these people to percieve things around them and help them distinguish basic activities. Through, the immense reformation happening in technologies, CognEye works to constantly upgrade their platform with respect to user-efficiency and user-favourable
